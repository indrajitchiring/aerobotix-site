Aerobotix Edtech Institute is Dibrugarh's first integrated Robotics, Drone, and AI education centre — a partner of Sparklebots, Pune, enhanced with a specialised artificial intelligence layer that bridges the gap between machines that move and machines that think.

Our Partner

Our Partner Sparklebots (erstwhile India First Robotics (IFR))

Headquartered in Pune, Maharashtra

Sparklebots, formerly known as India First Robotics (IFR), is one of the country's most established robotics education and R&D brands. With deep institutional partnerships, government collaborations, and corporate-grade project delivery, Sparklebots provides the proven curriculum, professional hardware kits, and industry credibility that form the backbone of every Aerobotix programme.

Sparklebots also carries an expanding international footprint, with presence across Canada, Qatar, Tanzania, Kenya, France, Germany, the UK, the USA, and Australia — bringing a wider global perspective into the learning ecosystem behind Aerobotix.
