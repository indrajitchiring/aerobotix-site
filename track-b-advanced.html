What Students Build in Advanced

  • Creo part design, assemblies, STL preparation, and functional 3D printed prototype workflows.
  • Servo-based motion builds, mechanical linkages, and improved robot-body integration.

  • Intro IoT prototypes with simple connected devices, dashboards, and live monitoring concepts.
  • Intermediate project integration that combines mechanics, electronics, and remote visibility.
  • Capstone builds that package Track B learning into polished, presentable robotics projects.
