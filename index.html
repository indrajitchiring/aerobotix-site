About Us

Built on India's strongest robotics foundation.
Powered by deep AI integration.

Aerobotix Edtech Institute is Dibrugarh's first integrated Robotics, Drone, and AI education centre — a franchise partner of India First Robotics (IFR), Pune, enhanced with a specialised artificial intelligence layer that bridges the gap between machines that move and machines that think.

Our Foundation Partner

India First Robotics (IFR)

Headquartered in Pune, Maharashtra

India First Robotics is one of the country's most established robotics education and R&D companies. With deep institutional partnerships, government collaborations, and corporate-grade project delivery, IFR provides the proven curriculum, professional hardware kits, and industry credibility that form the backbone of every Aerobotix programme.
